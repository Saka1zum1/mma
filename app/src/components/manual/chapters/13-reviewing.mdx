## Review mode

Review mode walks you through a set of locations one at a time in the panorama viewer, tracking which ones you have already looked at. Start it from a selection's menu (**Review selection**), from the duplicate finder, or with **Review selected locations** in the overview's selection bar.

- **Next / Previous.** <Kbd>Ctrl</Kbd>+<Kbd>Right</Kbd> and <Kbd>Ctrl</Kbd>+<Kbd>Left</Kbd>. **Save** also advances to the next location and marks the current one reviewed.
- **Delete.** Removes the current location and moves on.
- **Exit review.** The X in the review header leaves review mode without discarding your progress. The header shows your position (**3 / 50**) and how many you have reviewed so far.

Clicking another location's marker while a review is active jumps the cursor there if it is in the worklist; clicking off-list is a harmless peek that leaves your place untouched.

## Resuming a review

Reviews are saved sessions, so you can stop partway and come back later. Re-running **Review selection** on the same selection resumes its in-progress session rather than starting over. The **Reviews...** button in the overview's selection bar opens the session list.

<Img name="review-sessions.png" caption="The review sessions dialog: in-progress and completed reviews with progress bars." />

- **In progress / Completed.** Two tabs split active sessions from finished ones.
- **Resume.** Re-enters an in-progress session at the location you left off.
- **Select reviewed / Select unreviewed.** The two circle buttons turn a session's reviewed (or not-yet-reviewed) locations into a selection you can act on, without re-entering review mode.
- **Delete.** Discards the session.

While a review is active, two overlay selections mirror its progress on the map: **reviewed** and **unreviewed**, so you can see at a glance what is left.

## Duplicating a location

Press <Kbd>C</Kbd> in the location editor to duplicate the current location, then reframe the copy. Useful for capturing the same spot from multiple angles.

## Deleting

- **Single.** The **Delete** button or <Kbd>Delete</Kbd> in the editor.
- **In bulk.** Select locations, then press <Kbd>Delete</Kbd> on the map, use **Delete selected locations** in the overview's selection bar, or run it from the command palette.

## Viewport lock

Press <Kbd>V</Kbd> to lock the camera orientation relative to the road. Can be relevant for driving-direction specific camera artifacts.
