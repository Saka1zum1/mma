The **Controls** tab holds keyboard rebinding and movement speeds.

## Rebinding keys

- **Click to rebind.** Click a shortcut's current binding, then press the new key combination. Press <Kbd>Backspace</Kbd> or <Kbd>Delete</Kbd> while recording to clear it, or <Kbd>Esc</Kbd> to cancel.
- **Filter.** A search box narrows the (long) shortcut list.
- **Reset.** Each customized binding shows a **Reset** button; there is also **Reset all to defaults**.

## Conflict resolution

If you assign a combination already in use, MMA shows what it is bound to and offers to **Reassign** (which clears it from the other action). Conflicting rows are flagged with a warning you can click to jump to the other action. Some combinations are blocked: for example, a combination using <Kbd>Alt</Kbd> cannot shadow a navigation action, because <Kbd>Alt</Kbd> is the slow modifier.

## Per-map hotkeys

Each map can have its own hotkeys that override global bindings while the map is open. Two kinds of per-map binding are available:

- **Tag hotkeys.** Assign a key to a tag from its edit dialog. Pressing the key while a location is open toggles the tag.
- **Copy-to-map.** Assign a key to another map. Pressing the key copies the active location into that map.

## Movement speeds

- **Pan speed.** How fast the map pans with the keyboard.
- **Pano look speed.** How fast the panorama camera turns with the keyboard.
- **Alt slow-down.** The divisor applied while you hold <Kbd>Alt</Kbd> for fine control.
- **Pan to imported locations.** Whether the map jumps to newly imported locations.
