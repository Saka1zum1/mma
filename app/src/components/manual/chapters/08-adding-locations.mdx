## Click the map

Click an empty spot and MMA does not blindly drop a pin. It looks up the nearest Street View coverage, snaps to it, and creates the location at the corrected coordinates with the matching panorama. The candidate it keeps follows your per-map rules (official vs unofficial, camera quality, direction). See <ChapterLink id="loading-flags" /> for what gets pinned.

## Crosshair at center

Hold <Kbd>Enter</Kbd> to show a crosshair at the map center, then release to create a location there. It can be a bit janky, but is supported if you want to go mouseless.

## Paste coordinates or URLs

- **Paste a Google Maps URL** to drop a location at that point. Short links (`maps.app.goo.gl`) are resolved automatically.
- **Paste multiple URLs or coordinates** (one per line) to import them all at once. A staged preview appears before they are added (see <ChapterLink id="importing-into-map" />).
- **Paste into the search box.** Pasting a URL or coordinate pair into the place search control also imports the location.

<Note>

The **Min search radius** per-map setting controls how far the snap looks for coverage. In particularly dense areas, decreasing it can help find the exact pano you are clicking at.

</Note>
