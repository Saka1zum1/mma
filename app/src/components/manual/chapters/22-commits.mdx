export const title = "Commits & diffs";

A **Commit** bakes the current working changes into a permanent version. It is the durable checkpoint, distinct from the always-on autosave.

- **The Commit button.** In the bottom bar. Disabled when there is nothing to commit (<Kbd>Ctrl</Kbd>+<Kbd>S</Kbd>).
- **The pending diff.** Beside the button: **+added**, **-removed**, and **±modified** counts since the last commit.

<Note>

Undo/redo operates on individual edits within your session. Commits are the browsable, durable checkpoints. Use undo for "oops", use commits for milestones.

</Note>
