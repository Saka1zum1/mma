From the home screen, two icons in the bottom-right corner handle whole-library transfer.

- **Export.** Writes a single ZIP of every map (named with today's date). This is the recommended backup.
- **Import.** Restores from such a ZIP, or imports a single JSON, with the same preview and duplicate-detection flow described in <ChapterLink id="importing-existing" />.

<Note>

Make a backup before risky operations (database management, bulk deletes, large imports). Restoring is just a re-import of the ZIP.

</Note>
